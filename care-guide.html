Care Guidance

We know that you love your new Flat Friend and want to look after it the best way possible.

For this reason we have included our care guidelines within each product for you.

On our website, look up the product you would like to get guidance on and click on the "Product Safety & Care" tab.

If this section does not address your requirements you can send us an email to enquiries@flatfriends.com.au and we will address any concern you may have.
