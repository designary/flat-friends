  1. Welcome

    They are Flat Soft Toys and the animals are our friends. Hence the name Flat Friends. We hope by owning one of our soft toys you will learn to love, care for and learn about all animals that occupy our planet, particulary endangered species. 10% of our profit goes towards supporting animal charities.

    Top of Page

  2. Restrictions of Use

    Our soft toys are made from 100% Australian Lambskin, the bags that come with our soft toys are 100% cotton, and the information swing tickets are made from environmentally friendly boards.

    Top of Page

  3. Code of Conduct

    Our range of soft toys come in the shape of many different endangered animals. In developing a soft toy, we do a lot of research to find out about the animal. We manufacture the soft toy to match the real life animal as much as possible. Sometimes in doing this we encounter difficulties as we are converting a three dimensional animal into a two dimensional toy made from lambskin. It can take up to 2 years with many trials and errors before we get an animal right. Strict quality control is enforced in the manufacturing of each animal. The soft toy animal is kept to around.
